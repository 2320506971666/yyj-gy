About Me

I am a multidisciplinary artist based in Shanghai, exploring the intersection of traditional and digital media. My work examines the relationship between urban environments and human experiences, seeking to capture moments of contemplation and connection in our fast-paced world.

With a background in fine arts and experimental filmmaking, I create immersive visual narratives that invite viewers to pause and reflect on the beauty found in everyday experiences. My practice includes painting, photography, digital art, and short films.

My work has been exhibited in galleries across Asia and Europe, and I have collaborated with numerous brands and publications on creative projects that push the boundaries of visual storytelling.
